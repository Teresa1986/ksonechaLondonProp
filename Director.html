Chairman & Managing Director

Benzion Freshwater

Chairman & Managing Director

Benzion joined the Freshwater Group in December 1971 with primary responsibility for the Freshwater Group’s finances. He is a trained accountant and now has over 50 years of experience working in the property industry. In July 1976 he was appointed Managing Director and, additionally, became Chairman in July 1980.

Benzion is the son of Osias Freshwater who founded the property business in London’s East End after World War II having fled from his native Poland in 1939 to escape the holocaust.

Solomon Freshwater

Director & Head USA Operations

Solomon was appointed to the Board of the main companies in the Freshwater Group in January 1986. He is primarily responsible for the Freshwater Group’s operations in the USA and also has responsibility for the UK sales division. Solomon is the second son of Osias Freshwater and Benzion’s brother. He is also a Rabbi in the Jewish Northwest London community.
